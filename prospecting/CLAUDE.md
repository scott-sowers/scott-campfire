## Messaging Frameworks (from Scott Wiki — Sales Messaging Analysis)

### Pain Amplification (Use in Outreach)
- "Spreadsheet tax" — manual processes that scale linearly with headcount
- "Customization entropy" — NetSuite/legacy ERPs that become harder to maintain over time
- "15 days to close" — tie manual close processes to delayed business decisions
- "Consultant dependency" — ongoing cost of maintaining legacy ERP

### High-Intent Signals (Prioritize These Accounts)
- Recently raised Series B+ funding (scaling finance team)
- Job postings for Controller, VP Finance, or accounting roles
- Multi-entity or international expansion mentions
- IPO readiness discussions
- Known NetSuite or QuickBooks user at scale (501+ FTE)

### Outbound Language That Works
- Say "AI-native" not "AI-powered"
- Say "finance operating system" not "ERP"
- Use speed messaging: "live in weeks, not months"
- Name the enemy: "spreadsheet tax," "consultant dependency"
- Lead with a specific, relevant pain point — never generic
- Reference the $5M NetSuite Buyout Fund when targeting NetSuite shops
