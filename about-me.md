## What Campfire Does
Campfire is a **finance operating system** (position as "finance OS," not just "ERP") built AI-native from day one for high-growth companies. Three Pillars:

1. **Revenue Automation** — ASC 606 rev rec, usage-based billing, deferred revenue, automated revenue schedules, contract modifications
2. **Core Accounting** — Multi-entity consolidation with intercompany eliminations, multi-currency (triple-ledger FX), automated close management via rule engine
3. **Ember AI** — AI copilot trained on financial data: anomaly detection, natural language queries, predictive close timelines, automated journal entries

**Key value props I lead with:**
- Speed to value (weeks, not months) — in-house implementation team
- $5M NetSuite Buyout Fund (covers remaining contract costs)
- Self-service reporting (no IT dependency)
- API-first integrations (Plaid, Ramp, Brex, Rippling, Stripe, Mercury)
- Transaction-level data migration (not summary-level)
- SOC 1 & SOC 2 certified
