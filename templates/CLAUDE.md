# Templates Folder — Instructions

Reusable templates for common sales activities. Copy and customize — never edit originals.

## Available Templates
- `discovery-follow-up.md` — Post-discovery call email
- `demo-follow-up.md` — Post-demo email with materials
- `pricing-proposal.md` — Pricing discussion framework
- `handoff-intro.md` — Customer → Implementation team intro
- `cold-outbound.md` — Enterprise outbound email sequences
- `meeting-request.md` — Meeting request templates

## Template Rules
- Always personalize with specific details from the conversation
- Reference their company name, pain points, and next steps
- Never send a template without customization
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Include clear CTA in every email
